Yongsheng Jia

PhD in Pure Mathematics, The University of Manchester

Welcome to my academic homepage. I am a PhD student in Pure Mathematics at The University of Manchester. My research lies in geometric group theory and its connections with low-dimensional topology, surface dynamics, and symplectic geometry. I am particularly interested in understanding groups arising from dynamics and symplectic geometry through their actions on hyperbolic spaces. Recently, I have been increasingly attracted to the interactions between geometric group theory and probability, with a special focus on random walks on groups via their actions on hyperbolic spaces.
